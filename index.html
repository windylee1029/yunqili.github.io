About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in mathematics at Arizona State University. My research interests are in knot theory and geometric topology, especially Milnor-type triple linking invariants and topological measures for open curves in three-dimensional space.

I am advised by Eleni Panagiotou.

Research Interests

  • Geometric topology
